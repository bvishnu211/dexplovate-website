About Us

Dexplovate is a MedTech company using AI and hardware innovation to build purpose-driven solutions for the management and care of chronic diseases.

Our Mission

To build a suite of AI and hardware solutions that transform a patient's lived experience into actionable data, empowering medical professionals to deliver timely and precise care.

Our Story

Our journey began with a deeply personal experience during the COVID-19 pandemic, witnessing the silent struggle and helplessness of loved ones battling respiratory disorders. The pain of seeing them gasp for breath, often without a doctor nearby, ignited a fundamental question: "How can we map the invisible suffering that a chronic attack brings, especially when no medical professional is around?" This profound challenge became the genesis of Dexplovate. We assembled a dedicated team, united by a mission to reduce the burden of chronic diseases, starting naturally with asthma due to our close understanding of its impact.
