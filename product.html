Introducing AIR: Your Secure Companion for Proactive Asthma Care

Module can be kept on the chest or the spinal area

AIR – Asthmatics In Rescue is a purpose-driven MedTech solution designed to empower patients, caregivers, and medical professionals with the clarity and confidence needed to manage asthma effectively. Our mission, born from a personal journey, is to transform the daily struggle with asthma into a story of control and freedom.

The Problem We Solve: Bridging the Inter-Visit Gap

Asthma is a chronic disease where the severity is often dangerously understated, leaving patients undertreated and vulnerable to long-term health risks like airway remodeling. The critical gap between doctor visits is a period of guesswork, where symptoms are subjectively tracked and vital data is lost. AIR is engineered to fill this gap, providing objective, continuous monitoring that gives you a complete picture of asthma control.

How AIR Works: Effort-Independent Monitoring

Our compact, user-friendly device is simply placed on the patient's chest or spine when they feel symptomatic. It requires no complex technique or expertise, making it ideal for children and caregivers.

  • **Objective Symptom Tracking:** AIR’s hardware captures vital signs including heart rate, SpO₂, respiratory rate, and body temperature.
  • **Intelligent Lung Sound Analysis:** The software module uses advanced signal processing to accurately extract abnormal lung sounds like **wheezing**, transforming this data into objective clinical insights.
  • **Real-time Health Data:** All data is securely synchronized to a cloud-based platform, providing a **Symptom Control Assessment Sheet** based on GINA guidelines.
